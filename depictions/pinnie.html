Pinnie

Pin messages like iOS 14! Swipe left on a conversation to pin and hard press on a pin to get an unpin option. Note: On iOS 11/12 long hold on the pin and it will automatically unpin. This is a temporary solution.

Customization Options

  • Normal or Compact Layout
  • Set the size of Normal layout between small and large
  • Disable the "Drop Glow" and change it's opacity
