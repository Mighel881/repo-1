Muse

Muse is a collection of HSWidgets for music apps. Current supported apps are Spotify, Music, and SoundCloud. The Spotify widget is based on this concept and the Music widget is based on iOS 14's Music widget.
iOS 13+ only

Features:

  • Song Title, Artist, and Artwork
  • Spotify:
    • Next in Queue
    • Song Code
    • Play/Pause Button
  • Music and Spotify
    • Recently Played
    • Expandable
